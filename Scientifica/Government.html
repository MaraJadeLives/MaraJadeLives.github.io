Kingdom Of Scientifica

This is a test page. Things are subject to change.

We are a constitutional monarchy, and our head of state (Queen) is Github user MaraJadeLives.We use the semi-presidential system, where a President and Cabinet (made of ministers from different groups, and different interests). Ministers serve until the next election, unless they win again. The Cabinet is composed of Ministers for Astronomy, Technology, and more to come later. We were founded March 3rd, 2022. Ministers are responsible for educating the public about their area, and about Scientifica. We are also completely virtual, as we don't have any land, or a flag yet. Our Queen, besides being Queen, is also Webmaster, and Minister for Astronomy (and likely Technology, depending on who else she can recruit to join). Our flag is of an atom with electrons replaced by icons for various fields of science. Planned to go on the flag are

  • Leaf (representing Botany)
  • Star, or Telescope (representing Astronomy)
  • Microscope (representing Biology)
  • Volcano, or Rock (representing Geology)
  • and maybe some more??
