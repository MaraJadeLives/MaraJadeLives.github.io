My Gear

Cameras

I have a Canon 1100D (aka Rebel T3) DSLR. I chose this for big pixels, as it has 12MP on a crop sensor, vs most other crop sensor cameras having 18MP. I wanted big pixels to gather more light per pixel. It's my current camera for DSOs. I also have a Nikon Coolpix S8100, which I enjoy using for lunar work.

Len

I have a Mitakon CREATOR 135mm f/2.8, as it was the only 135mm lens from a non for sale by owner site that met my strict budget. I looked at getting a nifty 50, but then saw that the amount of sky that I could image was too big for some targets that I wanted to image. I chose the 135 just for framing of some targets.
