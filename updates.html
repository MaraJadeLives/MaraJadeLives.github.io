Update #1

Date: Jan 9th 2021

As those of you on Cloudy Nights might know, this month's astroimaging challenge is of M 42, or the Orion Nebula. As I had wanted to get a stack longer than 5 min, I got more data.I used just the new data, which ended up being ~30 min, which was split between 1.6 sec and 2 sec and f2.8-f4. I struggle with postprocessing some, but thought I'd post my final image so far
